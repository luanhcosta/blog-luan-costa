## About me

<img src="images/profile_photo.jpg" alt="Luan Costa" style="float: right; width: 220px; border-radius: 12px; margin: 2rem 0 1.5rem 2rem;">

**Senior Data Scientist | ML Engineer | MSc Computer Science**

<p style="text-align: justify;">I am a Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer with experience in designing, deploying, and scaling end-to-end machine learning systems in production environments. My work in the corporate sector has been centered on building intelligent data-driven solutions, with a particular focus on recommendation systems that deliver personalized user experiences.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">I have designed and implemented complete recommendation pipelines, from feature engineering and model development to deployment and monitoring. This includes deep learning–based architectures such as autoencoders, as well as classical machine learning approaches. My experience also involves architecting scalable infrastructure for model training and inference in cloud environments, ensuring reliability, reproducibility, and efficiency in real-world applications.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science, where my research focused on the intersection of quantum computing, generative models, and optimization. In addition to my core work on quantum algorithms, I developed expertise in training generative models based on decoder-only Transformer architectures. My research explored the use of these models to optimize the parameters of quantum circuits, specifically the Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA), with applications in portfolio optimization.</p>
